History

Formed in 1998, Viking Galaxy have been performing their unique brand of Space Viking Power Metal around the world for the last 22 years. After the success of their debut headline slot at Nice N' Sleazy's Glasgow in December 199, the band went on to record their first LP: First Contact. This record showcased soaring vocal melodies, pounding percussion and thunderous riffs.

Following the release of First Contact, Viking Galaxy went on to support the English Power Metal act Damnation Angels in Edinburgh as part of their UK Tour in March 2001 and Norwegian Power Metal band Gaia Epicus at Ivory Blacks in Glasgow in November 2001. Thus the career of Scotland's greatest space viking power metal band took off and they haven't looked back.
